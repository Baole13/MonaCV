**MonaCV** is an AI-driven web platform designed to help users analyze, improve, and optimize their CVs.  
The system evaluates user resumes, identifies missing skills, and suggests development paths based on AI insights.  
It also includes a **Virtual Interview feature** that simulates real interview scenarios and provides personalized feedback.

---

## Features

- **CV Analysis:** Upload your CV and receive AI-based evaluation on structure, clarity, and skill match.  
- **Skill Gap Detection:** Identify missing or weak skills and get suggestions to improve employability.  
- **AI-powered Recommendations:** Personalized learning paths and job preparation tips.  
- **Virtual Interview (AI Interview):** Simulate real interviews and receive feedback on performance, tone, and confidence.  
- **Dashboard:** Visualize your CV score, AI insights, and interview results in an interactive interface.
